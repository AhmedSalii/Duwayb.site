About Me

Learn More

My name is Ahmed Salih Alduwayb, and I am a metallurgical and materials engineering graduate from Ankara Yıldırım Beyazıt University. I have experience in designing and engineering models for PSA oxygen and nitrogen generation systems using 3D and 2D CAD software, such as Siemens NX, SolidWorks, and AutoCAD. I also have skills in material science and metallurgical analysis, as well as corrosion prediction and electrochemical detection.

I graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering from Ankara Yıldırım Beyazıt University in June 2023. I have completed internships at ETI MADEN ISLETMESI BIGADIC and AMEC INDUSTRIES, where I gained hands-on experience in using advanced analytical equipment and creating technical documentation. I have also worked as an independent contractor providing professional translation and interpreter services in Arabic, English, and Turkish.
