Hello and welcome to my website!

I'm Ahmed Salih Alduwayb, a metallurgical and materials engineer with a passion for creating and innovating. I graduated from Ankara Yıldırım Beyazıt University in June 2023, where I learned a lot about the science and technology of materials. I also gained valuable industry experience through internships at ETI MADEN and AMEC INDUSTRIES, where I designed and engineered models for gas generation systems and analyzed boron samples using advanced equipment.

I was born and raised in Tripoli, Libya, where I developed an interest in engineering and science from a young age. I moved to Ankara, Turkey, to pursue my studies and explore a new culture and environment. I enjoy learning new things and challenging myself as an engineer and person.

My main interest is in material design and characterization, especially for nanomaterials and composites that have novel properties and applications. I also like to work on personal projects that help me acquire new skills, such as programming in Python or using Fusion 360 software.

In my spare time, I like to read books on various topics, such as history, philosophy, or fiction. One of my favorite hobbies is playing chess, which I find relaxing and stimulating at the same time.

I hope you enjoy browsing through my online CV, where you can find more details about my projects and achievements. If you have any questions or comments, please feel free to contact me. I would love to hear from you.

Best regards,
Ahmed Salih Alduwayb
