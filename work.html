AMEC INDUSTRIES (Engineering Intern, June 2022 - July 2022)

At AMEC INDUSTRIES, I was involved in the design and engineering of PSA oxygen and nitrogen generation systems for various industrial applications. I used 3D and 2D CAD software, such as Siemens NX, SolidWorks, and AutoCAD, to create models and drawings of the systems and their components. I also prepared user instruction manuals and marketing materials in English and Arabic languages to support the company's global customer base. I helped with the translation of key departmental documents and internal communications to ensure clear and effective communication within the organization. I worked closely with the engineering team to identify and implement process improvements that increased the efficiency and productivity of the technical documentation process.

ETI MADEN ISLETMESI BIGADIC (Materials Engineering Intern, July 2022 - September 2022)

At ETI MADEN ISLETMESI BIGADIC, I gained hands-on experience in material science and metallurgical analysis. I assisted with the analysis of Boron samples using the X-Ray Fluorescence (XRF) device to accurately identify and measure the elemental composition of the samples. I documented the physical and chemical properties of Ulexite and Colemanite through standardized tests. I learned how to use advanced analytical equipment, such as XRD, SEM, and Raman devices, to characterize the structure and morphology of the samples.

Corrosion Rate Prediction in Oil and Gas Pipelines Using FreeCorp Software (Personal Project, March 2023 - June 2023)

For this personal project, I used Free Corp software to simulate and analyze the corrosion behavior of oil and gas pipelines under different pH and CO2 conditions. I studied the effects of various parameters, such as temperature, pressure, flow rate, water cut, and inhibitor concentration, on the corrosion rate of carbon steel pipelines. I discussed the corrosion mitigation strategies, such as cathodic protection, coating, and chemical inhibition, based on the simulation results. I also compared the performance of different corrosion models, such as NORSOK M-506, De Waard-Milliams, and CO2 Corrosion Rate Calculation Tool.
